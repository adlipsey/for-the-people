FOR THE PEOPLE

"...government of the people, by the people, for the people, shall not perish from the earth."

- Abraham Lincoln

Welcome to "For The People", a simple application that, from just your home address, can provide you with your current government representatives, upcoming elections with a map of your designated polling location, and a list of recent bill activity from Congress. At a glance, you can have all the information you need to know how, when, and where to get involved.
